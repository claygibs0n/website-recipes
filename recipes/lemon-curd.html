Lemon Curd

Lemon Curd is a tasty treat! It can be eated directly, dipped in, or used in other recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 5 Tbsp Butter (cut into small pieces)
  • 1/3 cup Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Simmer water in a double boiler. Do not place mixture over double boiler yet!
  2. On top of a double boiler, whisk together 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
  3. Gradually add 3/4 cup sugar, whisking until smooth and pale
  4. Add butter and lemon juice. Whisk to blend
  5. Place over double boiler. Stir constantly with wooden spoon until mixture bisibly thickens and coats the spoon
  6. Do not allow to boil or mixture will curdle!
  7. Remove from heat and transfer to bowl to cool. Cover and refridgerate

Notes

Will keep up to 3 weeks in fridge
