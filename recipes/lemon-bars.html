Lemon Bars

Lemon Bars are a tasty treat!

Crust Ingredients

  • 1/2 Lb Butter (Room Temperature)
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1/9 tsp Salt

Filling Ingredients

  • 6 Large Eggs
  • 3 cups Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Grated Lemon Zest
  • 1 cup Fresh Lemon Juice (Lemon juice has huge effect on taste!!!)
  • 1 cup Flour

Start the Crust

Preheat oven to 350 F

  1. Cream butter and sugar in electric mixer with paddle attachment
  2. Combine flour and salt, and with mixer on low slowly add to butter
  3. On well floured board, gather dough into ball
  4. Flatten dough and press into a 13" x 9" pan, building up 1/2" on all sides
  5. Chill for 20 minutes
  6. Bake crust for 15-20 minutes, until lightly browned. Cool on wire rack

The Filling

  1. Whisk together eggs, sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice and flour
  2. Pour over the crust and bake for 30-35 minutes, until filling is set
  3. Cool to room temperature

Notes

Will keep up to 1 week
